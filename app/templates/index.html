Welcome to Quiz Score Processor

This application helps you convert quiz scores from one scale to another.

Features:

  • Upload Excel or CSV files containing quiz data
  • Convert scores from original scale to a new scale
  • View detailed breakdown of scores for each student
  • Verify score conversion accuracy

How to use:

  1. Prepare your Excel or CSV file with quiz data
  2. Click on the "Upload" button below
  3. Fill in the quiz parameters (name, original max score, new max score, etc.)
  4. Upload your file and submit
  5. View the converted scores and details
